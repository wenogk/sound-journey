About

This website is an interactive 8D audio experience of a turbulent flight.

We are a group of passionate sound engineers who love to re-create realistic life events through original recordings and sounds found online. We have created this platform to make the user forget about everything they had in mind prior to clicking on our website so that they simply hear, see, and feel that they are a part of the small short-lived universe we create in our recordings. We strive to create an enjoyable experience during which users cannot differentiate between reality and the artificial world we pull them inside. Go ahead and check our Journey page out for many surprises that will take your breath away!
